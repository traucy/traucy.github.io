It's an alphabet made and stitched together with numbers from the Helvetica typeface.

01

Overview

This self-initiated project was a spontaneous design spree where I make the latin alphabet out of Helvetica number characters.

02

Restrictions

I cannot resize the original numbers.

I can cut, rotate and reflect the numbers.

Focus on creating lowercase characters.

No descenders.

Let the numbers' spirit shine through the characters.

Main Goal

Let the numbers' spirit shine through the characters.

My main goal was to not modify the characters to the point where numbers cannot be recognized. This led to difficulty defining a consistent grid since I did not want to modify the numbers too much. The new Neuemerals characters followed the horizontal grid of the original Helvetica character grid. I cut the numbers up and used them like puzzle pieces for each letter, you can see repetitive elements down below.
